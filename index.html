Hot Air Balloon

Riding a Hot Air Balloon is one of the most breathtaking activities in the world. From up above you can observe the dazzling Colombian Landscape.

This hot air balloon flight over the landscapes surrounding it lasts approximately 45 minutes, with plenty of time to admire the towering Andes and lush landscapes. Throughout the flight, you'll be in the safe hands of an internationally certified pilot, and coordinated by the air traffic controllers of the Airport.
